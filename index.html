The gap

When someone asks AI for a family lawyer in Mississauga, a financial adviser in Burlington, or a medspa in Oakville, the results aren't ranked by quality. They're ranked by entity coherence — how clearly and consistently the machine can classify what a business is, who runs it, and whether it's credentialed. Most local professional service businesses are incoherent to AI without knowing it. That incoherence is invisible. And it's costing referrals to businesses that simply have cleaner structured signals.
