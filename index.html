The gap

When a patient asks an AI for a safe clinic for lip fillers in Oakville, the results aren't ranked by quality. They're ranked by signal coherence — how clearly and consistently the machine can classify what a clinic is, who runs it, and whether it's credentialed. Most clinics are incoherent to AI without knowing it. That incoherence is invisible. And it's costing referrals to clinics that simply have cleaner data.
